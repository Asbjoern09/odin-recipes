Lasagna

This is a picture of finished lasagna

To make this, mix water, lasagnaplates, milk, minced beef and tomatoes

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Minced beef
  • Tomatoes
  • Water

Steps

  1. Put about a third of the minced meat in a bowl
  2. Add lasagnaplates until the bow is covered
  3. Add tomatoes and milk on top of the plates
  4. Repeat until you reach the top of the bowl and finish with plates
  5. Stick it in the oven at 200 degrees celsius for 45 minutes
